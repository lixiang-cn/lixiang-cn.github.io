Biography

I am a Ph.D. candidate in the First Clinical Medical College, Shandong University of Traditional Chinese Medicine, advised by Prof. Benzheng Wei. In addition, I also lead a brain image analysis group at the Center for Medical Artificial Intelligence of Shandong University of Traditional Chinese Medicine, working on developing intelligent neuroimaging analysis methods for multi-modal, multi-omics, and multi-center. Before that, I received my M.S. degree in biomedical engineering from the College of Intelligence and Information Engineering, Shandong University of Traditional Chinese Medicine, in 2021.

My research interests are primarily in developing advanced machine learning algorithms to assist radiologists in achieving fast and easy medical image analysis, including but not limited to brain image representation learning and bladder MRI image analysis.
